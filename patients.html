For Patients & Families

Patient advocacy groups we work alongside, and how you can take part in the research itself.

Dystonia Coalition investigators have been fortunate to work alongside patient advocacy groups around the world. Together, we:

  • Identify the research priorities that matter most
  • Choose the most promising projects to pursue
  • Design and carry out studies
  • Educate the wider community of patients and investigators

Take part in research

Participating in a study is one of the most direct ways to move dystonia research forward.

1. Find a study

Browse Our Projects to see what's currently enrolling.

2. Find a site near you

Check Clinical Sites for a participating location.

3. Reach out

Contact the site directly, or use our general contact form and we'll point you in the right direction.
