Who runs this

Meet Our Team

The Coalition works through four tiers of sites, each with a different role in the network.

Administrative core

Emory University coordinates the Coalition's operations, data management, and governance.

Project sites

Washington University in St. Louis, the University of New Mexico, and our partner site in India lead specific major studies.

Active sites

Sites that actively recruit patients and carry out studies across the consortium's research portfolio.

Affiliate sites

Sites that follow the Coalition's work, join our meetings, and stay connected without direct recruitment responsibilities.
