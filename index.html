Our goal, in one sentence

Our goal is to advance the pace of clinical and translational research in the dystonias to find better treatments.

Fifteen years of shared research have grown this into a working network of clinical sites, patient advocates, and investigators — this site collects what that work has produced, and where it's headed next.

15 years of dystonia research, together

Since 2009 the Coalition has grown from a focus on the most common adult-onset focal dystonias to include rarer childhood-onset and genetic forms — always guided by clinical sites, investigators, and the patient advocacy groups who help set our research priorities.
